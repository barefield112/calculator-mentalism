Force Settings
Configure the number this calculator will land on.
"=" presses since AC 0
"Force once" shows the number on the next = only, then the calculator resumes real math. "Force every =" keeps returning it until you hit AC.
"Type it in" animates the digits appearing one at a time instead of snapping in, for a more natural sell.
Birthday Mode
When someone multiplies a few numbers that matter to them (day, month, year), read their birthday back during the peek.
Day = input #
Month = input #
Year = input #
"Input #" refers to the order the numbers were typed in, same order used for the peek (7, 8, 9, 4, 5, 6...). During the peek, ÷ shows their birthday as m/d, × shows the year, − shows their age, + shows their star sign, and = shows the day of the week they were born.
